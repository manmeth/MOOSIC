# 🐄 Moosic

### *Your Daily Dose of Moo-sic.*

## Music should adapt to you—not the other way around.

Moosic is a next-generation music streaming platform built around one simple idea: **every listener deserves a personalized experience**.

Inspired by traditional music streaming services, we've reimagined the way people discover and enjoy music through AI-powered personalization, expressive customization, and a user-first approach. From intelligent playlist creation to customizable themes, every feature is designed to make listening more personal, effortless, and enjoyable.

---

# 🎵 Why Moosic?

Most music streaming platforms focus on delivering songs.

**We focus on delivering experiences.**

Music is personal, and your streaming platform should be too. Moosic learns your listening habits, understands your preferences, and evolves with you over time. Whether it's discovering your next favorite artist, organizing playlists effortlessly, or creating a space that reflects your personality, Moosic is designed to make every listening session feel unique.

---

# 🚀 What Makes Moosic Different?

### 🤖 AI Playlist Naming

Building the perfect playlist is easy—finding the perfect name isn't.

Moosic analyzes the mood, genre, and overall vibe of your songs to generate creative playlist names, making every collection feel complete.

---

### 🎨 Dynamic Themes

Your music has a mood, and your app should too.

Personalize your experience with custom themes or let Moosic automatically suggest color palettes based on your listening habits. Whether you're into calm acoustic evenings or energetic workout sessions, your interface can match your vibe.

---

### 🗑️ Playlist Recovery

Accidentally deleted your favorite playlist?

No worries. Moosic's Playlist Recovery feature lets you restore deleted playlists, ensuring your carefully curated collections are never lost.

---

### ❤️ Smarter Personalization

The more you listen, the better Moosic understands you.

Our recommendation system continuously learns from your listening habits, favorite artists, genres, and moods to provide suggestions that feel fresh, relevant, and uniquely yours.

---

### 🎧 Simplicity First

Listening to music should be effortless.

Moosic streamlines everyday actions like discovering songs, creating playlists, and navigating your library, making the entire experience clean, intuitive, and enjoyable.

# 🌟 Our Vision

At Moosic, we believe technology should enhance the way people connect with music—not complicate it.

Because music isn't just something you listen to—it's something you live.

# Built Around Digital Business Systems

Moosic was developed as part of a Digital Business Systems project to demonstrate how modern information systems work together to power intelligent digital platforms.

The application integrates:

- **Transaction Processing Systems (TPS)** — Capturing user interactions in real time.
- **Management Information Systems (MIS)** — Organizing and processing operational data.
- **Decision Support Systems (DSS)** — Delivering personalized recommendations through data-driven insights.
- **Enterprise Systems** — Managing business operations efficiently.
- **E-Business Platforms** — Creating a seamless digital experience for users.

Together, these systems transform everyday interactions into meaningful recommendations, intelligent automation, and an engaging user experience.
